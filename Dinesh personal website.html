Krishnamoorthy Dinesh

About

Hello ! I am a Post Doctoral Fellow at the Institute of Theoretical Computer Science and Communications, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. Before joining CUHK, I was a visiting student at the Faculty of Computer Science, Technion - Israel Institute of Technology hosted by Yuval Filmus.

I completed my doctoral degree from the Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Madras (2013-2019) advised by Jayalal Sarma. I completed my masters degree from CSE, IIT Madras (2011-2013) advised by Jayalal. Earlier, I did my undergraduate degree at the Dept. of CSE, NIT Calicut (2007-2011) under K Muralikrishnan

Research Interests

Circuit Complexity Theory, Complexity measures on Boolean Functions
