About

I am Dustin, a PhD student at the Department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, fortunate to be advised by Chandra Nair. I obtained my bachelor degree (first-class honour) under ELITE stream in Information Engineering from the same university in 2016.

My research interests are in Network Information Theory, Hypercontractivity and related inequalities and Distributed Source Coding. Besides, I like hiking and running.

Here is a short resume.
